Step 08 / 08

Paste your logo URL.

Don't upload — paste the direct image URL. Easiest way: hover over your logo on your website → right-click → Copy image address. Example: https://yoursite.com/wp-content/uploads/logo.png

Can't get the URL? Leave it blank. We'll auto-grab one from your website URL, and fall back to a wordmark if that fails.
