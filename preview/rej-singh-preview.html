Who Refers Consistently

The NOW ecosystem contains specific partner profiles whose clients are already primed for what you offer. These are the two highest-leverage referral relationships for Rej Singh.

01
Accountants & Business Advisers
Why they matter
Accountants see legal gaps early — shareholder issues during year-end, structure problems during restructures, tax exposure on sales. They're often the first to spot something that needs legal attention, and the last to have a lawyer they're confident referring to.
The trigger moment
A client mentions selling, restructuring, or bringing in a new shareholder — the accountant knows the documentation won't hold. That's the Rej moment.
What flows back
Your M&A and structuring work routinely surfaces tax and accounting questions. Warm lane back to the accountant, no friction.
02
Business Brokers & M&A Advisers
Why they matter
Brokers live with sale-readiness failure. Deals fall apart at due diligence when shareholder agreements are missing, IP is unclear, or contracts weren't assignable. They want a lawyer who cleans that up before they go to market — not during.
The trigger moment
Broker has a seller who "needs a tidy-up" — shareholder agreement, employment contracts, IP register. Rej is the fix, before the listing goes live.
What flows back
Your sale and acquisition work routinely needs a broker. Direct cross-referral lane — clean, bilateral, no overlap.
