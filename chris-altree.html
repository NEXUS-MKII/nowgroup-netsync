09 · Activation Sequence

Eye-2-Eye Guide

Meeting objective: Establish mutual referral understanding and identify immediate opportunities. Agenda: 1) Understand their client base and common challenges 2) Share specific tech pain points you solve 3) Identify 2-3 potential referrals in each direction 4) Agree on referral process and communication method. Key questions: What tech problems do your clients complain about most? When do you see businesses held back by IT issues? How do you prefer to handle referrals? Listen for: Specific client stories, timing of tech pain points, existing relationships with other IT providers. Close with: Exchange of 1-2 specific client situations and commit to follow-up timeline.
