08 · Partner Archetypes

Who Shares Your ICP

Business Accountants & Bookkeepers

They see financial symptoms of operational problems — messy cash flow, unclear reporting, manual processes creating errors

Sees early: Clients whose financials reveal operational chaos before the business owner admits there's a problem
Reciprocity: Their ongoing client relationships mean repeat referrals, my fixes make their accounting work easier
Business Growth Consultants

Growth amplifies operational problems — businesses can't scale if back office systems are broken

Sees early: Clients ready to grow but operationally unprepared, or growth stalling due to internal chaos
Reciprocity: Clean operations make their growth strategies actually executable, creating better outcomes for both
IT System Integrators

Technology solutions only work if underlying processes are clear — they see businesses wanting tech fixes for process problems

Sees early: Clients asking for software solutions when they need operational clarity first
Reciprocity: My process mapping makes their system implementations more successful, reducing project risk
Virtual Assistant Providers

VAs can't help businesses with undefined or chaotic processes — delegation requires operational clarity

Sees early: Businesses wanting to outsource but with processes too messy to delegate effectively
Reciprocity: My process work prepares perfect clients for their VA services, improving success rates
Industry-Specific Business Coaches

Coaches see the strategic picture but often find operational chaos blocking their recommendations

Sees early: Clients with good business ideas but poor operational execution preventing growth
Reciprocity: Operational fixes make their coaching more effective, creating better client outcomes and testimonials
